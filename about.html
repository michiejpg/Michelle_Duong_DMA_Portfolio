Michelle Duong is an aspiring digital media artist studying at SJSU as a recent transfer student. Starting her college journey from majoring in psychology, then to graphic design, she eventually landed herself in the world of digital media art.

There she found her love for creating stories, an alternate world within her videos or original coded projects. She has always been drawn to the kind of stories where it kept her on the edge of her seat, stories where she can solve the mystery along with the main characters. Something about the unknown called to her so that is what she based some of her projects on.

Within these works, she was able to express her imagination freely, to portray something odd, something mysterious, something eerie, simply because she has the freedom to do so.

She hopes to use the knowledge she gains from studying here at SJSU to one day create her own indie game or animation. One where the players go in with questions and leave with even more questions.
